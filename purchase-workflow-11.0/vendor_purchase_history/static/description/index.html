Vendor Purchase History

This Module will help you view Products you have purchased from your vendors in the past. It includes specific items you purchased and their cost. You can get insights about particular product- if it needs to be restocked, frequency of buying particular product, number of repeated vendors for a product, what are the items which we haven’t purchased yet and much more.

Prerequisites before Installation

No prerequisites are required before the installation.

Configurations

No Configurations are required.

Step-By-Step Guidelines

After Installation of the module go to Purchases --> Vendors. You can see the total number of Products purchased from particular Vendor. Also, under Company's Contact Form you can view the products bought by different members of your company. Let’s consider example of the Purchases History of Asustek Vendor.
